About Musicon

Musicon is a Music Recommendation System based on Facial Emotion Recognition.

The emotion recognition model is trained on FER 2013 dataset. It can detect 7 emotions. The project works by getting live video feed from web cam, pass it through the model to get a prediction of emotion. Then according to the emotion predicted, the app will display list of songs from Spotify through spotipy wrapper and recommend the songs by displaying them on the screen.
